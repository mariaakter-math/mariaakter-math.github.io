About Me

Click to view About Me

I am Maria Akter, a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa. My research focuses on commutative algebra in positive characteristic, with a particular interest in F-singularities. I am working under the supervision of Thomas Polstra. I earned both my B.A. and M.S. in Mathematics from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.
