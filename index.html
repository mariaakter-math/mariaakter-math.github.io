About Me

Maria Akter

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa. My research focuses on commutative algebra in positive characteristic, with a particular interest in F-singularities. I work under the supervision of Thomas Polstra. I earned both my B.A. and M.S. in Mathematics from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.
